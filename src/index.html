Just Report It is not just a normal spam “reporter“, instead ,it focuses on the root of the problem and reports these domains to the registrar effectively blocking these spammers from ever sending spam from that email address again.

Open-source

Just Report It is a free and open-source, cross-platform addon to easily report spammers to the domain registrar.

Efficient, fast and scalable

By utlizing serverless technologies, we are able to quickly scale and adapt to any demand we may have and quickly fetch the registar's abuse information.

Multi-platform

Just Report It’s addon is available for Thunderbird and Outlook.
